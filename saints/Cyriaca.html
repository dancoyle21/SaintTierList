Saint Cyriaca
Martyr
BornRome, Roman Empire
Died249
Venerated inRoman Catholic Church, Eastern Orthodox Church[1]
CanonizedPre-congregation
Major shrineSanta Maria in Domnica, Rome, Italy
Feast21 August Roman Catholic
19 May Eastern Orthodox[1]

Cyriaca, also known as Dominica, was a Roman widow, and patroness to St. Lawrence, and eventually suffered martyrdom. St. Lawrence used her home in Rome to give food to the poor. Cyriaca suffered martyrdom, by being scourged to death for her faith.[2]
