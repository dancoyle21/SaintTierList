Arsenius (Matseyevich)

Arseny Matseyevich

Metropolitan Arsenius (secular name Alexander Ivanovich Matseyevich, Russian: Александр Иванович Мацеевич; 1697, Volodymyr-Volynskyi – February 28, 1772) was bishop of the Russian Orthodox Church, metropolitan of Rostov and Yaroslavl who protested against the confiscation of the church's land by Empress Catherine II in 1764. He was deprived of his office and was imprisoned in a fortress until his death.

He was canonized in 2000.
