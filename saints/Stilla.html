Stilla is a genus of sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the family Raphitomidae.[1]

Distribution[edit]

This genus occurs off New Zealand.

Species[edit]

Species within the genus Stilla include:

  • Stilla anomala Powell, 1955[2]
  • Stilla delicatula Powell, 1927[3]
  • Stilla fiordlandica Fleming C., 1948[4]
  • Stilla flexicostata (Suter, 1899)[5]
  • Stilla paucicostata Powell, 1937[6]
