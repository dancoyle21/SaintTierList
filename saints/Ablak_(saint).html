Ablak (saint)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ablak
Venerated inEthiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church
Feast30 May

Ablak is a saint of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church. His feast day is kept on May 30.
