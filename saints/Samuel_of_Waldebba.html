Attributesriding a lion

Samuel of Waldebba (Ge'ez ሳሙኤል ዘሀገረ ወልድባ sāmū'ēl za-hagara waldibbā) was a 15th-century Ethiopian monk and ascetic of the Ethiopian Orthodox Church.
