Eusebius of Mâcon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Saint Eusebius of Mâcon was a 6th-century bishop of Mâcon in France.[1][2]

He is known to have attended the Second Council of Mâcon in 581 and Third Council of Mâcon in 581 and 585.[1]

He is considered a saint of the Roman Catholic Church.
