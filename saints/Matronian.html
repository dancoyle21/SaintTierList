Saint Matronian
Madonna and Child and Saint Matronianus (detail). 15th century (the Madonna) and 16th century (Saint Matronianus) fresco in the nave of San Nazaro Maggiore in Milan.
Venerated inRoman Catholic Church
FeastDecember 14

Saint Matronian (U.S.) (Italian: San Matroniano) was a hermit of Milan. It is recorded that Saint Ambrose enshrined Matronian's relics in the church of San Nazaro Maggiore in Milan.
