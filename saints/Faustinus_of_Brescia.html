Faustinus of Brescia
Bishop of Brescia
Born4th century
Died15 February 381
Feast15 February

Saint Faustinus (died about 15 February 381 A.D.) was bishop of Brescia from c. 360, succeeding Saint Ursicinus. His feast day is 15 February.

Tradition claims that he was a descendant of Saints Faustinus and Jovita, and that he compiled the Acts of these two martyrs.
