Blessed Agnes Takea was a Japanese Roman Catholic martyr. Agnes was married to another Catholic layperson, Bl. Cosmas Takeya Sozaburō. They were beheaded, with Bl. Charles Spinola and companions, during the "Great Martyrdom" at Nagasaki. She was beatified in 1867.[1][2][3][4]
