The Diocese of Caorle or Diocese of Calina (Latin: Dioecesis Caprulana seu Caprularum) was a Roman Catholic ecclesiastical territory in the town of Carinola in the Province of Caserta in the Italian region Campania.[1][2] It was suppressed in 1818 to the Diocese of Sessa Aurunca.[2]

History[edit]

1099: Erected as the Diocese of Carinola[2]
1818 June 27: Suppressed to the Diocese of Sessa Aurunca[2]
1968: Restored as the Titular Episcopal See of Carinola[2]
