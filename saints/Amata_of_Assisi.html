Saint Amata of Assisi
BornAssisi, Italy
Died1250
Venerated inRoman Catholic Church
Feast20 February

Amata of Assisi was a Poor Clare nun. After a youth misspent, Amata became very ill and was healed by her aunt, St. Clare. With this miracle she changed her life and entered the Poor Clares, later becoming an acquaintance of St. Dominic.[1]
