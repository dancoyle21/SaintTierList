Oliva of Brescia

Died138

Major shrineSaint Afra's Church, Brescia, Italy
Feast5 March

Saint Oliva (or Olivia) (†138) was martyred under Hadrian; her relics are venerated at Saint Afra's Church, Brescia. Her feast day is 5 March.
